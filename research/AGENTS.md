# Research

This project uses the **Athena** tiered research wiki. Research flows through
`unconfirmed/` -> `confirmed/` -> `consolidated/` -> (optionally) `design/`,
where each tier is a higher trust level than the one before.

**If you're about to write, move, or promote any research or design content,
load the `athena` skill first.**
